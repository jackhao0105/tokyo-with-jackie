Old building in Jimbocho

This old building caught my eye as I was walking to the station from the sketch above (if you know the area, you'll notice it was a bit of a long walk). I started this sketch on one day and finished it the next time I was in the area. I love this part of town, so it was only about a week before I was back there. I stood for this one since I was on a busy street corner. This is in the same sketchbook as the one above, and done with the same tools.
